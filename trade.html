MANUFACTURER OF LUBRICANTS

ALCO is the largest manufacturer of lubricants in the Caucasus with an annual capacity of 100,000 metric tonnes.

Trade

ATechnology is the key driver of our manufacturing success. All plant operations are automised and remain free of human intervention.

Certain practical aspects of our business complement our manufacturing efforts and ensure that we can deliver our products to consumers. One of the most important practical aspect is trade. As a manufacturer geared towards export we have found ourselves continuously engaging in physically trading certain products. Physical trade involves a number of procedures that come together for the completion of a certain project. Having systemised these procedures and to a certain extent automised management through software application, we have increased our access to new markets and increased sales volume and value, which in turn delivered better overall results for our investors. Once we established a network that we used to distribute products manufactured in-house, we came to the decision to use our expertise to trade products manufactured by other companies as well. We have an excellent team of individuals who are more than capable of handling the operational aspects of considerably large shipments, which includes the mitigation of risk associated through hedging.
