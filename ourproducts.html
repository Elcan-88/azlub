MANUFACTURER OF LUBRICANTS

ALCO is the largest manufacturer of lubricants in the Caucasus with an annual capacity of 100,000 metric tonnes.

Our products

Technology is the key driver of our manufacturing success. All plant operations are automised and remain free of human intervention.

Base oil

The base oil trade was an instinctive addition to our portfolio because we stock large volumes of it for manufacturing use. Through steady large volume purchases, we can achieve prices that are unavailable to regular buyers. Furthermore, we have necessary infrastructure to bunker and pack these materials and provide on demand. These two advantages created the opportunity for price arbitrage, which we capitalised on to build a base oil trading portfolio. Since 2018, our portfolio has tripled in size and our relationships with suppliers have grown stronger, allowing us to supply bulk amounts of base oils.

Auto-chemicals

Certain products are complementary to what we currently sell on international markets. Having commissioned the lubricant manufacturing facility in 2016, we realised that our customers also had a demand for auto-chemicals. At the time, we did not commissioned the de-ionised fluids factory, and therefore could not manufacture these goods ourselves. Therefore, in order to avoid saying no to our customers we began private label manufacturing these products under our brand names. This acted as a blessing in disguise as today we are able to use the de-ionised fluids factory to produce products that are more profitable while we outsource the manufacturing of other products.

Additives

Another organic addition to our portflio were lubricant, fuel and polymer additives that we procure in large amounts and store for in-house use. Through years of experience in manufacturing lubricants, fuel additives and HDPE canisters, we gained insight on industry trends and minimised costs that created a price-arbitrage opportunity. We capitalised on this opportunity by obtaining official and exclusive distribution for a number of countries from internationally reputed suppliers. The establishment of this system coupled with our network of loyal customers allows us to grow our additive portfolio year on year.

Urea

As an environmentally conscious company aiming towards zero emissions, we also manufacture and trade products aimed at reducing emissions of diesel engines. Emission reduction fluids used for maintenance in Euro 5 and 6 engines are solvents of high-grade urea otherwise known as carbamide. Having access to the product and stocking significant amounts for in-house use, we realised that there was an opportunity for price arbitrage with this product also. We capitalised on this opportunity further by using our insight on industry trends and minimising costs to deliver an excellent product to our customers at competitive prices.

Ethylene glycol

We manufacture and delvier antifreeze and coolant fluids for industrial and commercial uses. In doing so, we use a significant amount of ethylene glycol which we store in large volumes. Our relations with some of largest refiners of this synthetic material in the region gives access to a stable supply of the product throughout the year. Whereas, we began procuring ethylene glycol for in-house manufacturing uses our re-sell portfolio has now outgrown our ready product portfolio. This is another instinctive addition to our portfolio that enjoys significant success and that our customers are extremely happy with. We have installed the necessary infrastructure in recent years to allow us to pack ethylene glycol in a number of bulk forms, which has significantly boosted sales volumes.
