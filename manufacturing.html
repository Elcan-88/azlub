MORE MANUFACTURING STORIES

Specialty lubricants

Our specialty lubricants are designed to exhibit and maintain the parameters specified by our customers’ technical instructions even under the most demanding conditions. This segment of our business combines field analyses with laboratory research in order to develop products that are suitable to the needs of individual clients, which deliver clear benefits. In general, the specialty lubricants we manufacture, due to our focus on customer requirements, has delivered great benefits in terms of less maintenance costs through longer change intervals and the prevention of dilapidation through optimal operation, as well as less material costs through transparent and fair pricing based on market figures and trends.

Commercial lubricants

Due to high demand by our customers, we have developed three brands, which are manufactured and delivered using our state-of-the-art European standard facility. These brands are traded with over 20 countries primarily through private contractors who trade under their own names, but who are nonetheless members of our team and compliant with our strict rules for service. Our products are distinct in their level of quality due to supreme base oil and additives used in manufacturing. They have earned a positive reputation in international markets, therefore allowing us to increase outputs year over year and enter new markets.

Deinoised fluids

In 2019 we established our first de-ionised fluids blending and packaging facility. This facility is used to manufacture a number of products including, glycol mixtures for commercial and industrial usage, concrete admixtures, maintenance fluids for commercial vehicles and industrial applications, and simple concentration or dilution processes based on the requirements of our customers.

Specialty chemicals

We use of de-ionised fluids blending and packaging facility to deliver specialty chemicals to customers who are interested in specific solutions. Similarly, to specialty lubricants we use our R&D laboratory to carry out specific tests based on field findings and develop products base on these specific requests. In the past our products have delivered significant benefits to our customers particularly because our focus allows us to achieve lower blending cost and a higher quality product than the customer would achieve by simply blending themselves or by applying a one-size-fits-all commercial solution.

Facilities

Lubricants manufacturing facility comissioned in 2016

2016 marks the beginning of our journey with the constuction and comissioning of a lubricants manufacturing facility with an annual capacity of 35,000 mt. The facility was designed, built and comissioned by a team of German, Italian and Russian engineers. It is the largest factory in the Caucasus region in terms of capacity. Having reached maximum operation capacity in 2020 the facility was expanded to produce an annual 50,000 mt.

Quality control and R&D laboratory comissioned in 2018

In 2018, we finished constructing our second, relatively larger and more modern laboratory facility, equipped with the technology to conduct analyses on petro-chemicals and petroleum products to the precision of 1 ppm. The design of this 500 meters squared facility was designed to ensure workplace safety and the well-being of the scientists who work here. All equipment is highly specialised to safeguard personnel and prevent accidents.

Deionised fluids facility comissioned in 2019

In 2019, we constucted and comissioned a de-ionised fluids factory with an annual capacity of 6,000 mt. The facility was designed, built and comissioned by a joint team of German and Turkish engineers. This facility currently operates at maximum output levels, and is dedicated to maufacturing specialty items which are in short supply in international markets.

Blow moulding facility for HDPE canisters in 2020

As part of ALCO’s horizontal integration strategy we consturcted and comissioned a facility to blow mould our own HDPE canisters. This allows greater flexibility in manufacturing, lower costs, shorter lead times, and a higher degree quality control. The facility operates as an individual unit and serves in-house demand as well as other customers.
